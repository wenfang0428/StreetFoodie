ABOUT

Street Foodie is your digital passport to the world’s street food culture. We bring together the flavors, stories, and traditions behind iconic dishes from different countries – from smoky satay in Malaysia to comforting oden in Japan and sweet bubble tea in Taiwan with a hint of sour tang som tum in Thailand. More than just food, each dish reflects a community’s history and way of life. At Street Foodie, we invite you to explore, taste, and celebrate the joy of street food from around the globe.
