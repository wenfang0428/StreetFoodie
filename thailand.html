THAILAND

Pad Thai

Stir-fried rice noodles with tamarind sauce, tofu, eggs, shrimp or chicken, bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts.

Som Tum

A spicy and tangy salad made from shredded green papaya, chili, lime, fish sauce, tomatoes, and peanuts.

Moo Ping

Juicy marinated pork skewers grilled over charcoal, often enjoyed with sticky rice.

Mango Sticky Rice

Sweet ripe mango served with glutinous rice cooked in coconut milk, topped with sesame seeds.

Khao Soi

A Northern Thai curry noodle soup with egg noodles, coconut milk, curry broth, chicken or beef, and crispy fried noodles on top.
